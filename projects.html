Late in 2009 I started to dabble in iOS development. Maybe it was the lure of the AppStore, or the tedium of building back office web applications, I'm not sure, but iOS quickly changed from an interest into a passion.

After working as a Lead iOS developer at ChaiONE Inc for a couple years, I decided it was time to go out on my own. Well, it turns out it's really lonely on the road, so I reassessed, and decided to go back to ChaiONE. That's where you'll fine me today, and I coulnd't be happier.

I got my start building ASP pages back in 1999. That progressed into 8-9 years of ASP.net consulting work. When MVC was released I fell in love with the framework and started reading up on its roots. That led me to Ruby on Rails, which is where focused for a few years.

I started to dig into client-side web development in 2008, and quickly jumped on the jQuery bandwagon. That blossomed into full-on javascript love. I'm currently playing with Node.js and learning all the goodness that it has to offer. I've also jumped head first into Ember.js. It's where I spend most of my time these days.

Game Plan HQ

Built for enterprise sales & marketing professionals who need real-time access to the most effective sales & marketing collateral on-the-go, Game Plan is a break-through mobile sales enablement and presentation tool.

Game Plan connects to popular cloud and private cloud storage solutions such as Box, SharePoint, DropBox, and Amazon S3. No need to move your files to yet another storage service; Game Plan HQ connects to them right where they are.

Invite others (such as an entire sales team) to link to your stored files and view relevant materials on their own iPads.

See what everyone is using, what's working, and what's not, as Game Plan collects near-real-time analytics on every file's usage by everyone on your team. Marketing and sales departments can finally see what's going on in the field and make intelligent decisions on what is needed.
